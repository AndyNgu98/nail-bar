About Us

We're a family run business located in the heart of Sydenham, just a short walk from the train station.
We have been working in the nails and beauty industry for over 13 years.

Our Services

We offer an extensive range of nail and beauty treatments for both men and women.
All our treatments are offered by well trained and certified staff.

Manicure

Your hands are soaked to soften the skin and cuticles, followed by a buff down to even out your nails, and then a file to ensure they are all the same shape and size.


Pedicure

A therapeutic treatment for your feet that removes dead skin, softens hard skin, shapes and treats your toenails.


Shellac

Nails are treated with chip-resistanct Shellac polish that gives up to two weeks of wear, an incredible mirror shine and has an easy, non damaging removal.


Acrylic Nails

Great for those looking to add length and strength to their nails, acrylics can also create a larger, more evenly shaped canvas for those who love intricate nail designs.

custom nails are available on request.
